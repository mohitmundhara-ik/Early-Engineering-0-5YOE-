FAQ

      
Questions, answered.

    

    

      
      
      
    

    

      

        
What does the 14-week program cover?
▼
The program covers DSA, Agentic AI engineering, System Design, and career readiness. DSA and Agentic AI are live sessions; System Design is self-paced and included in the curriculum. You also get 2 live mock interviews with MAANG+ engineers and full career support.

        
Is it live or self-paced?
▼
Both. DSA and Agentic AI run live. System Design is self-paced so you can complete it around your schedule.

        
What does it cost?
▼
The program fee is ₹70,000, with 0% no-cost EMI from ₹5,834/month. Scholarships and financing options are confirmed live in the free webinar.

        
Is there a refund policy?
▼
Yes. 14-day refund window from your first class, same-day processing.

        
What are "application opportunities"?
▼
Application opportunities are subject to eligibility criteria and successful completion of the program as per Interview Kickstart's terms and conditions. They are opportunities to apply for roles and do not constitute an offer or guarantee of employment, placement, or any specific outcome.

      

    

    

      

        
Who is this course for?
▼
This program is designed for tech professionals with less than 5 years of total work experience, currently working in - or transitioning into - the following domain roles: Android Developer, Back-end Engineer, Cyber Security, Data Engineer, Data Science, DevOps Engineer, Embedded Software Engineer, Front-end Engineer, Full Stack Engineer, iOS Developer, Machine Learning / AI Engineer, Site Reliability Engineer, and Test Engineer / SDET / QE. If you are a fresher, recent graduate, or in the early stages of your tech career, this program is built for you.

        
Do I need to be working already?
▼
No. Final-year students, new grads, and engineers in their first roles are all welcome. The program is designed for 0-5 YOE engineers at any stage.

        
What programming background do I need?
▼
You should have basic programming knowledge in any language. The program assumes you can write simple code and understand core CS concepts. No prior AI/ML experience is required.

        
How do I enroll?
▼
Start by booking a free webinar. Our team will walk you through the program, answer your specific questions, and help you decide if it's the right fit before you commit.

        
When does the next cohort start?
▼
Cohort dates and seat availability are shared live on the free webinar. Seats are limited - book your spot to lock in your place.

      

    

    

      

        
What will I be able to build after the program?
▼
You'll be able to design and deploy Agentic AI systems, solve DSA problems at interview level, architect systems at scale, and confidently handle the full interview loop at top tech companies.

        
Will this help me get a job at a top tech company?
▼
The program is built specifically for interview performance at MAANG+ and top-tier product companies. You'll do 2 live mock interviews with real engineers from these companies, plus get resume, LinkedIn, and application support.

        
What does the career support include?
▼
Resume review, LinkedIn and GitHub optimization, an AIQ score and personalized roadmap, unlimited AI mock interviews, access to a recorded mock interview library, and up to 5 application opportunities when you meet the eligibility criteria.

        
How long does it take to see results?
▼
The 14-week program is designed so you're interview-ready by the end. Most learners start applying within 4-8 weeks after completing the program. Individual timelines vary based on background and effort.
