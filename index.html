FAQ

      
Questions, answered.

    

    

      
Who is this track for?
▼
Engineers building the foundation of their careers — final-year students, recent graduates, and those in their first engineering roles.

      
Do I need to be working already?
▼
No. Final-year students, new grads, and engineers in their first roles are all welcome.

      
Is it live or self-paced?
▼
Both. DSA and Agentic AI run live. System design and electives are self-paced.

      
What does it cost?
▼
We share pricing and financing options live in the free webinar.

      
What are "application opportunities"?
▼
When you meet the eligibility criteria, the Careers Team puts your profile forward for relevant openings. Opportunities to apply — not a job guarantee.

      
Is there a refund policy?
▼
Yes. 14-day refund window from your first class, same-day processing.
